3. Create any viz that communicates the point.¶

What is the point of this viz, in one sentence?

  • This is a pretty generic question, which could be adapted for the end of any lab. Here are a couple of leading questions that could be used:
    • Create one visualization that communicates your results. Include a descriptive title, and assume that this visualization is all you will be showing stakeholders.
    • Create two visualizations of different types using base Matplotlib and/or Seaborn settings. Which is better, and why? (E.g. compare a pie chart and a bar chart)
    • Visualize the distribution of some variable from this lab. Discuss your choice of graph and any changes you made to default settings. Summarize the shape of the distribution in one sentence. (E.g box plot vs histogram, number of bins)
    • You are interviewing with a non-technical HR professional. You've just explained your capstone project. Create one or two visualizations that represent the performance of your model. (E.g. true-vs-predicted values, false-positive-rate plotted against threshold values, even a confusion matrix would suffice. An ROC curve would be inappropriate here.)
